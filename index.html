Mabawa

Creating Sustainable Social Change in Nyamyumba, Rwanda

Who are we?

Mabawa is a nonprofit organization that was created to get the village of Nyamyumba back on its feet again, after the devastation of the Rwandan Genocide. We focus on improving seven areas of the community: health, agriculture, education, water, housing, microcredit, and beekeeping. We also do our best to begin integrating pygmies into Nyamyumba.

Work Thus Far

So far, Mabawa has built multiple schools and houses, provided Nyamyumba with doctors, distributed livestock, supplied clean water, helped the people launch successful businesses, and promoted prosperity through beekeeping. For more details read my next medium post.

NGO Work

NGO stands for non-governmental organization; its purpose is to raise awareness and create good change, whether it be for a disease, an area, a group of people, etc. However, it's been brought to my attention that some believe NGO's do more harm than good.
